Our Story

At Reframe, we are dedicated to empowering professionals across the Middle East by providing services and resources in Arabic.

Reframe MHS was established with a vision to provide culturally relevant mental health services across the Middle East. We began our journey with a focus on understanding the unique experiences of our communities.

Launched our first comprehensive training programs for mental health professionals. We established partnerships with local organizations and began building our reputation for excellence in professional development.

Expanded our services across the Middle East region, establishing the Reframe Hub as a professional meeting space and developing specialized consultation services for corporations and NGOs.

Launched our Mental Health Lab to advance research and innovation in mental health care. Introduced professional translation services and continued to grow our impact across the region.
