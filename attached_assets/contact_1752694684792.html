Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What languages do you offer services in?

We provide services in both Arabic and English, with a focus on culturally relevant content for the Middle East region.

How can I register for training programs?

You can register through our events page or contact us directly. We'll provide you with all the necessary information and registration details.

Do you offer online training sessions?

Yes, we offer both in-person and virtual training sessions to accommodate professionals across the region.

What types of organizations do you work with?

We collaborate with healthcare institutions, NGOs, educational organizations, and government agencies across the Middle East.

Are your training programs certified?

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all our training programs, recognized by professional bodies in the region.

How can I book the Reframe Hub?

You can book our meeting spaces through our hub page or contact us directly with your event requirements.
