Our Services

Individual Support

  • Confidential counseling sessions (short-term, solution-focused).
  • Psychological first aid for staff exposed to crises.
  • Coaching for stress management and resilience.
  • Referrals and follow-up support when needed.

Group Support

  • Stress management sessions (post-crisis, burnout, workload).
  • Peer support groups (safe space to share experiences).
  • Resilience circles using art, storytelling, and movement.
  • Team-building with therapeutic methods.

Workshops & Training

  • Stress management & burnout prevention.
  • Resilience skills for high-pressure environments.
  • Emotional regulation & coping strategies.
  • Trauma-informed care for high-stress contexts.
  • Mental health awareness & stigma reduction.

Organizational Services

  • Psychosocial risk assessments.
  • Monitoring and evaluation system for staff well-being.
  • Management reports: risks, staff needs, recommendations.
  • Mediation during workplace conflicts.

Specialized Interventions

  • Thematic staff-care days (art, music, mindfulness).
  • Support for managers on leading with mental health awareness.
  • Transition support for staff leaving organizations.
  • Emergency response packages for crises.
